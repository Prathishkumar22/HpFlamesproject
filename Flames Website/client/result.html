The FLAMES game

A popular way to determine the compatibility between two people based on their names.

The game involves the following steps:

Remove all common letters between the two names.

Count the total number of remaining letters.

  • F: Friends
  • L: Lovers
  • A: Affectionate
  • M: Married
  • E: Enemies
  • S: Siblings
